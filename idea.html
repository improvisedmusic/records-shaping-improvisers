How the idea came about

The field of improvised music over the past 70 years has been far less thoroughly explored than, for example, electronic music, jazz, or contemporary composed music. While teaching and studying improvised music in Basel, the idea emerged to provide a kind of historical overview of important movements, bands/ensembles, musicians, and albums in improvised music.
In attempting to create a timeline of important albums, it became clear how personal such a selection is. For this reason we opened up this survey to collect a bigger variety of perspectives.
