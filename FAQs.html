FAQs

Should all the records be of improvised music?

No. As improvised music collects different influences, the records you write can be inherent to any genre that was in any way inspiring for developing your practice as an improvising musician.


Should I write down my favourite records?

This inquiry is not about favourite records, but about the records that most influenced your practice as an improvising musician. They might be the same, but maybe not.


Improvised music has to be experienced live! Don't you think this research will provide only a partial overview of improvised music scenes?

Yes, we agree that music often has a stronger impact when experienced live than on recordings. Nevertheless, we believe that the outcome of this project will provide an inspiring archive, hopefully highlighting meaningful connections across different scenes and generations.


How will the research be shared?

We'd like to share the research in an interactive way, allowing results to be filtered by categories such as age, nationality, identity, label, or release date. We'd also love to implement an interactive map, though the final format will be decided once sufficient data has been collected.


When will the research be shared?

The project was launched on May 17th 2026. We'd like to collect at least 200 replies. Considering the time required to gather and process the data, we hope to share the first results during winter 2026/27.


I don't have time now. Can I do it in a couple of months?

Yes, probably. It would be ideal to receive most responses by September 2026, but the survey will remain open indefinitely, including after the publication of results. The archive will be updated twice a year.


Can I share the project? And how?

Yes, that would be very helpful. Feel free to send it to people who may be interested and share the project link:
https://improvisedmusic.github.io/records-shaping-improvisers/


Can I participate anonymously?

Yes. There is no need to provide your name or email address. On the other hand, we would appreciate it if you could fill in the optional information regarding age, nationality, and identity.


If I put my name, who will know it?

Your name will be known only to organizers. All results will be shared anonymously.
