Records shaping Improvisers

With this survey we aim to create an overview of influential albums in improvised music.

Improvisers [1] With the word “improvisers” we refer to musicians who, even when using compositions or concepts, do not understand improvisation primarily as a strategy to produce variations over a fixed form (namely stylistic improvisation, as in jazz, blues, rock, classical, baroque, etc.). of all generations, genders, and backgrounds are addressed.

The aim is to create an open, evolving archive growing from within the scene.
